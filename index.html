Our Story

36 Years of Taking Sri Lanka to the World

Founded in 1988, Skyways Travel Solutions began as a passionate small venture with one mission: to make world-class travel accessible to every Sri Lankan. Over three and a half decades, we've grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted travel companies — but our heart remains the same.

"We don't just book trips. We craft experiences that become lifelong memories."

From our offices in Colombo, we serve travellers across every category — families seeking beach holidays, executives needing flawless business trips, adventurers chasing thrills, and pilgrims seeking peace.
